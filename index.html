Our Story

Soup2Bowl was born from a love of comfort food and a belief that good soup brings people together. Founded by Shelley Wirt, our kitchen started as a small home operation and has grown into a full-service catering company trusted by hundreds of families and businesses.

Every recipe is made from scratch using the freshest seasonal ingredients. We never use concentrates or artificial flavors — just honest, delicious food.

Whether it's a lunch meeting for 10 or a wedding reception for 300, we deliver warmth in every bowl.
